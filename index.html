About Us

The story behind Full Protein

Our Story

Where Italian Elegance
Meets Arabic Tradition

Full Protein was born from a vision — to create a dining experience that bridges two of the world's greatest culinary traditions. Located in the heart of Jeddah, our restaurant brings together the refined techniques of Italian cooking with the bold, aromatic flavors of the Arabian Peninsula.

Every dish on our menu tells a story of fusion and harmony. Our chefs masterfully combine Italian classics like handmade pasta and risotto with Arabic staples such as za'atar, sumac, saffron, and premium Saudi dates. The result is a menu that feels both familiar and excitingly new.

We source the finest local ingredients and import select Italian specialties to ensure every plate meets our exacting standards. From our wood-fired pizzas topped with shawarma-spiced chicken to our saffron-infused linguine, every bite is crafted to surprise and delight.

Chef Marco Al-Harbi

Executive Chef & Founder
